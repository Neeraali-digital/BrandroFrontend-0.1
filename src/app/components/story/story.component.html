Meet the Founder

Meet the Founder

Hima has always believed that every brand has a story waiting to be told. Fascinated by the way narratives can shape perception and create lasting connections, she built her career helping brands discover their voice and share it with the world.

Armed with an MA in Media and Public Relations from the University of Leicester, UK, Hima returned to Kerala with a vision: to create a space where strategy meets creativity and communication becomes art.

That vision became The Brandro - a PR and branding house dedicated to helping brands show up with purpose and impact.

For Hima, branding isn't just about visibility - it's about meaning, authenticity, and leaving an impression that lasts well beyond the first glance.
